Plainsboro Library Classes

For students near Plainsboro, NJ, we offer library classes for free!

In our library classes, we teach children essential programming skills through project-based learning, guiding them to create their dream games using Scratch or Python.

Classes are held at the Plainsboro Public Library every two weeks. Walk-ins are welcome. We have a limited supply of them of laptops, so it is suggested to bring your own laptop.

Visit us at 9 Van Doren St, Plainsboro, NJ 08536 / 10:45am-12pm.

1-on-1 Classes

Our biweekly library classes can only push your child so far. If you or your child wants:

  • Personalized Classes
  • Virtual or In-Person
  • Custom Curriculum
  • Train for a Competition (e.g. USACO, ACSL)
  • Obtain a certification (e.g. PCEP, CS50 AI, etc.)

Consider booking 1-on-1 classes with our top-notch instructors. They can help your child reach their full potential in no time.

Pricing

As mentioned previously, our library classes are 100% free!

Our 1-on-1 classes cost $50/month for 1 class/week. With 4 weeks per month, that comes to $12.5 per class.

If you are interested in 1-on-1 classes, please contact us!
